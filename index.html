Dolleh

Wolof for power, strength, and backbone — the support that lets something greater stand tall.

Our Story

A partner who stands beside you, not above you.

Dolleh was built on a simple belief: farmers carry enough already. The land, the weather, the markets, the labour — the weight is real. Dolleh exists to share that load.

We provide the equipment, the inputs, the logistics, and the know-how that let farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses across The Gambia and Senegal focus on what they do best — growing.

Mission

Carry the operational weight of agriculture so farmers can focus on growth.

Vision

A West Africa where every farmer has the backbone of a reliable partner.

Values

Trust, strength, community, and quiet professionalism in every field we serve.

Approach

Local knowledge paired with modern, dependable agricultural infrastructure.

What We Do

Full-weight support,
start to harvest.

From the first tilled row to the last delivered sack, Dolleh's services are built to remove friction at every stage of the farming season.

01

Tractor Hire

Well-maintained, modern tractors and implements available on flexible terms — ploughing, harrowing, and harvesting without the cost of ownership.

02

Agricultural Inputs

Quality seeds, fertilizers, and crop protection products sourced and delivered reliably, season after season.

03

Farm Support

On-the-ground operational help — labour coordination, equipment maintenance, and seasonal planning support.

04

Logistics

Reliable transport of harvests, inputs, and equipment between farms, cooperatives, and markets.

05

Farmer Training

Practical training in modern, sustainable farming techniques delivered by people who understand local conditions.

06

Advisory Services

Honest guidance on crop choices, financing, and operations — strategic backbone for cooperatives and agribusinesses.
